About Merrin

MerryNotesDesigns was founded by Merrin Sharples-Moore.

Hi, My name is Merrin. I am 25 years old. I started teaching myself graphic design about half way through my History degree, when I was 19. I have been trading online since September 2014. Since exhibiting at Top Drawer S/S20 I now offer my products for wholesale. Please contact me if you're interested in becoming a stockist, or order via creoate.com. I hope you like my designs. I like to combine bold colours with striking images of animals, plants and other things I find pretty. I also love a good pun. If you are interested in ordering a custom design or logo, or working on an ongoing freelance project, just visit my 'Get in Touch' page. Thank you for reading!
